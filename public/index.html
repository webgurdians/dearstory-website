Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

+

From the time you share your story and photos, we take 5–7 working days to design and review, then 3–5 days for printing and delivery. Total: 10–15 days across India.

How do I share my photos and story details?

+

After placing your order via WhatsApp or our form, we'll send you a simple link or WhatsApp chat to collect your photos, names, memories, and any specific story ideas. No technical knowledge needed.

How many revisions do I get?

+

Digital plan (₹499) gets unlimited revisions. Printed plan (₹1999) gets 2 revisions before we send to print. Premium Gift Box (₹2999) gets 3 revisions before printing. We show you the full digital preview before anything is printed.

Can I see the storybook before it's printed?

+

Absolutely. We always share a full digital preview for your approval. Nothing goes to print without your confirmation.

What size is the printed storybook?

+

Our standard storybooks are A4 size (21cm × 29.7cm), printed on premium 200 GSM matte art paper with a hardcover binding.

Can I write my own story or do you write it for me?

+

Both! You can share your own story/text and we'll format and illustrate it. Or share your memories and our team will write a beautiful personalized story for you — included in the price.

What if I'm not happy with the final product?

+

We work with you through revisions until you're satisfied with the digital version before printing. If the printed book has a quality defect or damage during delivery, we'll reprint and reship at no cost.

Do you do bulk or corporate orders?

+

Yes! We offer bulk pricing for weddings, corporate gifting, school events, and more. WhatsApp us at +91 9046105790 for a custom quote.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We currently accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), bank transfer, and cash on delivery is not available. We'll send you a payment link after confirming your order on WhatsApp.

Is my personal data and photos safe?

+

Your photos and story details are used solely to create your storybook and are never shared with any third party. We delete all files from our systems within 30 days of order completion.
