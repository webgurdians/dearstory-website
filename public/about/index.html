About DearStory

Every family has a story worth telling. We help you tell yours.

Why DearStory Exists

Every family has a story worth telling. A child's laugh that echoes in the hallway. A couple's first argument over who gets the last samosa. A mother's quiet sacrifice that nobody noticed but everybody felt.

These stories live in our hearts — but they fade. Slowly, with time.

DearStory was born with one belief: the most meaningful gift you can give someone is their own story, beautifully told.

We create fully personalized, illustrated storybooks for real people — kids, couples, parents, friends — crafted with care, printed with love, and delivered to your door across India.

What We Promise You

  • Every book is 100% unique. No templates. No copy-paste.
  • We write it, illustrate it, print it, and deliver it — you just share your memories.
  • You approve before we print. Always.
  • If it's not perfect, we make it right.
